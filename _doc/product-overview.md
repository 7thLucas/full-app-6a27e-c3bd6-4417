# GymPulse — Product Overview

## What It Is
GymPulse is a gym operations platform built for independent gym owners and small gym chains. It centralizes member check-in tracking, equipment maintenance scheduling, and trainer schedule management in a single web-based admin dashboard — giving owners the operational visibility they need to increase member engagement and maximize equipment uptime.

## Primary Users / Personas
- **Gym Owners / Operators** — the primary buyer and decision-maker; needs operational oversight, KPI summaries, and proactive alerts across all three pillars
- **Front Desk Staff** — manages day-to-day member check-ins, handles member lookups, and monitors daily attendance
- **Trainers / Personal Trainers** — views and works within their own schedule, session assignments, and availability windows
- **Maintenance Staff** — logs equipment issues, marks maintenance tasks complete, and tracks service history per machine

## Positioning
GymPulse replaces the disconnected spreadsheets, paper sign-in sheets, and group-chat scheduling threads that most independent gyms rely on today. It is purpose-built for gym operations — not a generic facility management tool adapted for fitness — and is designed to be immediately navigable for non-technical owners.

## Brand & Tone
- Energetic, confident, action-oriented — the voice of a product that respects operators' time
- Clean and direct; no jargon, no bloat
- Color palette: primary orange (energy and action), slate (professional credibility), emerald accents (positive outcomes)
- Product name: **GymPulse**

## Core Feature Pillars

### 1. Member Check-in Management
- QR-code or staff-assisted digital check-in at the front desk
- Real-time attendance dashboard showing current occupancy and daily check-in counts
- Member visit history and engagement trend tracking over time
- Automated low-engagement alerts when a member's visit frequency drops below a configurable threshold (e.g., 14+ days absent)

### 2. Equipment Maintenance Tracking
- Equipment inventory registry with per-machine status, location, and service history
- Scheduled maintenance reminders (e.g., quarterly service cycles)
- Ad-hoc issue reporting by staff with severity tagging
- Equipment uptime metrics and overdue maintenance alerts
- Full service log per equipment item from installation to decommission

### 3. Trainer Schedule Management
- Weekly trainer shift planner with at-a-glance daily and weekly coverage view
- Session and class assignment per trainer
- Trainer availability management so staff can flag open or blocked windows
- Automatic scheduling conflict detection and alerts
- Owner-facing coverage summary so no shift goes unassigned

## Strategic Principles
- **Simplicity first** — gym owners are not technical; the dashboard must be immediately navigable without training
- **Proactive alerts** — surface problems before they become crises: overdue maintenance, absent members, scheduling gaps
- **Data as motivation** — transform raw operational data into engagement trends, uptime scores, and coverage metrics that owners can act on
- **Unified operations** — three core pillars in one place eliminates tool-switching and data silos

## Scope (v1)
- Web-based admin dashboard, desktop-primary
- No native mobile app in v1
- Member-facing features (self-service check-in kiosks, member portal) are out of scope for v1 and planned for v2
- Multi-location management is a v2 feature

## Business Goal
Increase member engagement and equipment uptime for gym owners — turning reactive, crisis-driven gym operations into a proactive, data-informed management practice.

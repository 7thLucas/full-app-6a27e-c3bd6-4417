The Case for GymPulse

Most gyms operate without a clear
picture of what's actually happening.

The Problem
▸ Members quietly stop showing up — nobody notices until they've already cancelled their membership.
▸ Equipment fails at peak hours because maintenance runs on memory, not a system.
▸ Trainer schedules live across texts, whiteboards, and personal calendars — gaps and conflicts are invisible until it's too late.
The Solution
▸ Real-time check-in tracking with automated low-engagement alerts — catch at-risk members weeks before they cancel.
▸ Equipment maintenance logs with scheduled service reminders — prevent breakdowns before they strike.
▸ Centralized trainer planner with conflict detection — every shift covered, every session accounted for.
